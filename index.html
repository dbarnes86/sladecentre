About

North Dorset

North Dorset's premier independent arts centre — free to visit, welcoming to all.

10+
exhibitions per year
Free

2023
opened our doors

The Slade Building sits at the centre of Gillingham's market square — a handsome old building with a new purpose. We're an independent arts centre and gallery dedicated to showing the best of contemporary and regional art, alongside a café that gives you a reason to stay a while.

We're proudly independent, volunteer-supported and entirely community-run. Our programme of exhibitions changes throughout the year, bringing new artists and new work to North Dorset.

We also sell art books, locally made greeting cards and small prints.
