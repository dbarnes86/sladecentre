About

A place for art
in North Dorset

The Slade Building sits at the centre of Gillingham's market square — a handsome old building with a new purpose. We're an independent arts centre and gallery dedicated to showing the best of contemporary and regional art, alongside a café that gives you a reason to stay a while.

We're proudly independent, volunteer-supported and entirely community-run. Our programme of exhibitions changes throughout the year, bringing new artists and new work to North Dorset. Entry to all exhibitions is free.

We also sell art books, locally made greeting cards and small prints. Sign up to our newsletter to hear about upcoming shows.
